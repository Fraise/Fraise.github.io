**Style:** American pale ale

**Est. OG:** 1,047

**Est. FG:** 1,006

**Est. Alc.:** 5.4%

**Batch vol.:** 10L (2,5 gal.)

**Yeast:** OYL-071 Lutra

**Malt:**

- 1,4kg pilsner malt
- 600g vienna malt
- 100g wheat malt
- 100g flaked barley
- 50g honey barley

**Hops:**

- 5g Sabro @ 15mn
- 5g Idaho 7 @ 15mn
- 10g Sabro @ flameout
- 10g Idaho 7 @ flameout
- 10g Sabro @ dry hop
- 10g Idaho 7 @ dry hop

**Mash temp.:** 67C in 15L (4 gal)

**Boil:** 1h

**Notes:** A light, slightly hoppy pale ale with a malty finish and a smooth mouthfeel.
